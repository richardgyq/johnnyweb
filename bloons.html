Bloons are your main enemy in the game. They will follow a certain path and try to go through the exit. In the corner of your sceen you will notice a number of hearts, for every bloon that goes through you lose one. If it hits zero you lose. Some bloons are slow, while others are fast. More importantly though, most bloons have multiple layers. blue bloons have 2 layers, green 3, yellow 4,etc. Also there are special properties such as camo, lead, regrow and moab class bloons.
