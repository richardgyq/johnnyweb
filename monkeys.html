Monkeys are towers in this game and will shoot all bloons that come in range. They each cost different amounts and have three upgrade paths. You can use two of three upgrade paths, and there are 5 upgrades in each. but only one direction can go all the way while the other can only go up 2. Also there can only be one teir 5 of that monkey.

Dart Monkey

The dart monkey is the basic monkey that you start with. It throws normal darts that can pierce 3 bloons. In the first path, his first upgrade is called sharp shots and makes it so he monkey can pierce 1 more bloon. Next is razor sharp shots which makes it pierce 2 more bloons. After is spike-o-pult which converts its darts into a catapult that shoots slower but can now pierce 23 bloons. For tier 4 is the juggernaut, and makes it so it shoots a giant spiked ball which is also able to pop lead and can pierce 101 bloons. The ultra juggernaut hurls a giant spiked ball that slits into 6 smaller balls and can pierce 1614 bloons. In path 2 his first upgrade is quick shots and makes it so he shoots 15% faster. Then is very quick shots which makes it so it shots 33% faster. Triple darts make it shoot 3 darts. Super monkey fan club is an ability that makes makes up to 10 dart monkeys including itself into super monkeys for 10 seconds. Elite monkey fan club makes up to 15 dart monkeys into plasma super monkeys for 10 seconds. In path 3 the first upgrade is longer range which increases range. Next is enhanced eyesight that increases range and gives camo detection. Crossbow transforms his darts into a crossbow which can pierce 4 bloons and can pop 3 layers. Sharp shooter does crits every few shots and can pop 6 layers. Crossbow master shoots really bad and can pop many layers.
