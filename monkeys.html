Monkeys are towers in this game and will shoot all bloons that come in range. They each cost different amounts and have three upgrade paths. You can use two of three upgrade paths, and there are 5 upgrades in each. but only one direction can go all the way while the other can only go up 2. Also there can only be one teir 5 of that monkey. There are 4

Dart Monkey

The dart monkey is the basic monkey that you start with. It throws normal darts that can pierce 3 bloons. In the first path, his first upgrade is called sharp shots and makes it so he monkey can pierce 1 more bloon. Next is razor sharp shots which makes it pierce 2 more bloons. After is spike-o-pult which converts its darts into a catapult that shoots slower but can now pierce 23 bloons. For tier 4 is the juggernaut, and makes it so it shoots a giant spiked ball which is also able to pop lead and can pierce 101 bloons. The ultra juggernaut hurls a giant spiked ball that slits into 6 smaller balls and can pierce 1614 bloons. In path 2 his first upgrade is quick shots and makes it so he shoots 15% faster. Then is very quick shots which makes it so it shots 33% faster. Triple darts make it shoot 3 darts. Super monkey fan club is an ability that makes makes up to 10 dart monkeys including itself into super monkeys for 10 seconds. Elite monkey fan club makes up to 15 dart monkeys into plasma super monkeys for 10 seconds. In path 3 the first upgrade is longer range which increases range. Next is enhanced eyesight that increases range and gives camo detection. Crossbow transforms his darts into a crossbow which can pierce 4 bloons and can pop 3 layers. Sharp shooter does crits every few shots and can pop 6 layers. Crossbow master shoots really fast and can pop many layers.

Boomerang Monkey

The boomerang monkey throws a boomerang that can pierce 4 bloons. In the first path, the first upgrade is improved rangs which just lets you pierce 8 total. Then is glavies which can pierce 13 and move faster in the air. Next is glavie ricochet which will bounce from bloon to bloon and pierce a total of 50 bloons. For fourth teir is M.O.A.R glavies will bouce from a bigger range and pierce 100 bloons. Finally is glaive lord, it has three glavies that pop lead and camo and has infinite pierce. In the second path is Faster throwing first and it makes it throw 20% faster. Next is faster rangs and make the rangs faster. Then is bionic boomerang which throws multiple boomerangs and throws really fast. For fourth teir is Turbo charge and it is an ability that makes him throw even faster. Finally is Perma charge which makes it so that it always throws really fast and it's ability makes it so it can pierce more and pop more. In the third path is long range rangs and increases the range. Then is red hot rangs give the power to pop lead and frozen bloons. Kylie boomerang is next and it is thrown in a straight line and pops 2 layers. For fourth teir is Moab press and it hits maoabs multiple times as well as knock them back sometimes. Finally is Moab Domination, which hits moabs from any distance and pops 11 layers.
